About Remember5

Purpose of this Website:

Remember5 is a web appliction that will help Muslims pray their 5 daily prayer on time by sending notifications at each of the daily prayer times. This application will also provide those muslims with many other features fun and engaging features such as he ones listed below:

Feature 1: Study Resources

Come learn more about the religion with readings, lessons, and quizzes.

Feature 2: Prayer Schedule

Always stay up to date on the 5 daily prayer times.

Feature 3: Feedback

Give us your review of our site, any suggestions, and a 5-star rating.

Feature 4: Preferences

Have complete control of your preferences, and change them anytime at your convenience.

Feature 5: Chatroom

Chat with all those using the website, and set up prayer groups with those around you.

Feature 6: Map

A map to see where other Remember5 users are, if you are close, can meet up to either socialize or pray togeather.
